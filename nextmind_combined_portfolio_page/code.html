About NextMIND

NextMIND is an innovative platform leveraging AI to provide personalized mental health support. Our mission is to make mental wellness accessible and effective for everyone.

Features

Discover how NextMIND's features can help you achieve a healthier, happier mind.

AI-Powered Responses in Talk

Receive tailored recommendations and insights based on your unique mental health profile.

Nextcloud API + AI Proxy

Secure handoff between Nextcloud APIs and our AI Proxy for low-latency inference.

Smart Floating Widget

Docked/minimized bubble that expands to a full panel; persists across pages and tabs.

Real-time Notifications

Cross-tab sync using BroadcastChannel/WebSocket; unread badges and subtle toasts.

Our Tech Stack

Our tech stack is meticulously chosen to deliver a seamless and responsive user experience. The interface, crafted with React/Next.js and styled with Tailwind CSS, comes to life with Framer Motion animations. All of this is presented within our signature dark theme, accentuated by neon gradients and sleek glassmorphism effects that highlight the technology driving NextMIND. The backend leverages Nextcloud APIs, bridged via an AI Proxy, with WebSocket ensuring real-time communication, making the platform both powerful and robust.
